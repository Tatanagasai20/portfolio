About Me

Passionate about building robust cloud infrastructure

I am Naga Sai Kumar, a passionate AWS DevOps Engineer with over 3 years of hands-on experience in designing, implementing, and managing enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure. My journey in technology began with a fascination for automation and scalability, which naturally led me to the world of DevOps and cloud computing.

Throughout my career, I have specialized in building robust CI/CD pipelines, containerizing applications, orchestrating microservices with Kubernetes, and implementing infrastructure as code using Terraform. I believe in the power of automation to transform how organizations deploy and manage their applications, reducing manual errors and accelerating time-to-market.

Currently at Priacc Innovations Private Limited, I lead cloud infrastructure initiatives where I architect and implement scalable AWS solutions for enterprise clients. My work involves designing multi-tier architectures, implementing security best practices, optimizing cloud costs, and ensuring high availability of production systems. I have successfully migrated legacy applications to cloud-native architectures, resulting in significant performance improvements and cost savings.

My previous experience at Ticketmaster India exposed me to high-traffic, mission-critical systems where I learned the importance of reliability, monitoring, and rapid incident response. At UJR Technologies, I honed my skills in containerization and infrastructure automation, building the foundation for my DevOps expertise.

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest cloud technologies and DevOps practices. My approach combines technical excellence with strong communication skills, enabling me to bridge the gap between development and operations teams effectively. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can contribute to building innovative solutions that drive business value.
